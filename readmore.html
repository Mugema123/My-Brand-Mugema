            nation and Africa at large. Let elaborate a bit on this, Patriotism or national pride 
            is the feeling of love, devotion and sense of attachment to a homeland and alliance with 
            other citizens who share the same sentiment. This attachment can be a combination of many 
            different feelings relating to one's own homeland, including ethnic, cultural, political or
             historical aspects. Unfortunately, it is totally different for many Africa leaders because

              world this means that the leaders are corrupted. So, some Africa leaders have to change the 
              behaviour in order to make a prosperous progress in our beautiful Africa.
            
            To sum up, Africans need to get a clear thoughtful and benefits of Africa integration in order
            to put together force, resources and skills for making Africa super-power. And also, all leaders
            must have love for the countries to be able to avoid corruption in their homeland and help other
            countries in this continent. With those above, Africa will be super-power undoubtedly.
